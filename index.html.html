Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

1. What data I collect

  • Billing information — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy (email, payment method). I never see or store your card details.
  • Your financial data (income/expense entries) — stored securely in Confluence's database (via Supabase), tied to your account. It's private to you: only your signed-in account can access it, enforced at the database level. It persists across sessions and devices — you'll see the same data whenever you sign back in with your email.
  • Account access — sign-in uses passwordless email links ("magic links"). I don't store or see a password. Your subscription status is checked against Lemon Squeezy's records to confirm access.
  • Email address — if you join the mailing list on the landing page, your email is stored via Formspree, a third-party form service, solely to send you occasional product updates. This is separate from your account email.

2. How your data is used

Billing data is used only to process your subscription. Your income/expense entries are used only to show you your own dashboard — I don't access, analyze, or share individual account data. Mailing list emails are used only to send you updates about Confluence — never sold or shared with third parties for marketing.

3. Third parties involved

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and billing (Merchant of Record)
  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication for your account and financial data
  • Resend — delivers sign-in emails to your inbox
  • Cloudflare — network and security layer that routes traffic to this site
  • Formspree — email collection for the waitlist form
  • Netlify — website hosting

4. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting me — this permanently removes your income/expense entries from the database. You can also request deletion of your email from the mailing list separately. Billing data deletion requests should go through Lemon Squeezy directly, as they control that data.
